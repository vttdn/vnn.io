About Vincent Tantardini

I’m Vincent Tantardini, a designer & developer focused on clear interfaces, lasting ideas, and building simple products that solve real problems. Frames is my latest work. A notebook for analog photographers to log and organize shots, then reintegrate notes as metadata into scanned images. Bridging film and digital for those who shoot on film. I value focus over noise, usefulness over trends, and making things that stand the test of time.
