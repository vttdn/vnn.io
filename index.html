About Vincent Tantardini

In design, development, or documentation, I’m drawn to the in-between—where technology meets craft and ideas take shape. My path spans product design, front-end engineering, and photography, each informing the next. I’m interested in tools that not only work but resonate—extensions of how we think, create, and observe the world.

In recent years, I’ve focused much of my energy on building Frames, a thoughtfully crafted app for film photographers to log, organize, and reflect on their analog shooting process. What started as a personal need became a full-featured ecosystem for photographers who still shoot on film but live in a digital world.

I believe in software that disappears into the background, interfaces that reward attention, and products that age gracefully.
