About Vincent Tantardini

I'm Vincent Tantardini, a product designer, a front-end developer, a photographer, a writer, a curious mind, a careful observer, a builder of quiet tools, ... Frames is my latest work. It's a notebook for analog photographers—designed to log, organize, and reflect on each shot with clarity and purpose. A bridge between film and digital, made for those who still shoot on celluloid. I care about what lasts. About tools that stay useful, and ideas that stay true.
